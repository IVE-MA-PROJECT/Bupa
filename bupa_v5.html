Good Private Health Insurance does come at a cost
 
We appreciate that any increase in insurance premiums can be tough, especially right now. But we want to help ensure we can continue to deliver quality services to our 4 million customers in Australia. Increases in premiums are related to the rising costs of providing health care – partly driven by things like our ageing population, medical advances, inflation and an increase in people using hospital services.
 
We will continue to provide ﬁnancial hardship support if you need it
 
We know that some customers are still feeling the ﬁnancial effects of COVID-19. If you think this increase will cause you genuine ﬁnancial hardship, ﬁnd out more to see how we can help. It’s also important to regularly review your cover to ensure it still meets your needs, and we can easily help you with that too.
 
And we’re offering more support in 2021
 
As we start our recovery out of COVID-19, we acknowledge there is a signiﬁcant role for the health system in providing care and support. While physical distancing helped to prevent people from contracting COVID-19, it created other problems, including isolation, loneliness and anxiety.
 
Because you have Mental Health included in your Extras cover, you will automatically receive an additional $200 per [person/policy] for claims related to Psychology or Counselling services, until 31 December 2021. Find out more to see how this will help you, along with other wellness related new features.
